Word for Today

Walking in Glorious Light

"But you are a chosen generation, a royal priesthood, a holy nation, His own special people, that you may proclaim the praises of Him who called you out of darkness into His marvelous light." As children of God, we are called to carry His light into every space we occupy — our homes, workplaces, and communities. Today, let your life reflect the glory of the One who called you.
